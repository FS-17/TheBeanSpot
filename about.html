Saudi Arabia's Best Coffee Journey

Founded in 2010 by coffee enthusiasts with a vision of bringing world-class specialty coffee to KAFD. Now recognized as Riyadh's top coffee destination, we've set the standard for coffee excellence in Saudi Arabia through relentless dedication to quality.

We source the finest arabica beans globally and blend them with local Arabian tradition, perfecting our craft to deliver unique flavor profiles that have made us KAFD's premier specialty coffee destination. Our master roasters bring decades of experience to every batch.

What We Stand For

Sustainability

We prioritize ethical sourcing partnerships with farmers and eco-friendly practices across our KAFD location to ensure a better future for coffee.

Community

We foster connection and belonging through every brew, creating a welcoming space for Riyadh's coffee lovers to gather and share experiences.

Quality

Every cup reflects our dedication to excellence, from bean selection to the final pour. We've been recognized as Saudi Arabia's premium coffee destination.
